Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SarkariZone ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use SarkariZone:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you register or sign in via Google.
  • Profile Information: Age, gender, education level, and exam category you are preparing for.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, jobs bookmarked, and features used within the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalised job listings and exam notifications based on your preferences.
  • To manage your account, bookmarks, and premium subscription.
  • To improve app performance using Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.
  • To show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob (free users only).
  • To process referral codes and premium subscriptions via Google Play Billing.

3. Third-Party Services

SarkariZone uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication, Firestore database, Analytics, Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob – Advertisements for free users
  • Google Play Billing – Premium subscription payments

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with service providers necessary to operate the app (listed above).

6. Biometric Data

If you enable the Biometric Lock feature, your fingerprint or face data is processed entirely by your device's operating system. SarkariZone does not collect, store, or transmit any biometric data.

7. Children's Privacy

SarkariZone is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information within the app.
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
